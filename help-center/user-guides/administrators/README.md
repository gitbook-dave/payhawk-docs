## Core responsibilities

### Managing users and teams

Navigate to **Settings → Users** to:

- Invite new users by email
- Assign roles: **Administrator**, **Accountant**, **Employee**
- Create and manage teams (used for spend policies and reporting)
- Deactivate users who have left the company

### Setting spend policies

Spend policies control what employees can spend, on what, and up to how much. Go to **Settings → Spend Policies** to create rules based on:

- Team or individual
- Expense category
- Merchant type
- Amount per transaction or per month

{% hint style="info" %}
Policies are enforced at the point of spend — a card transaction that would breach a policy is declined automatically.
{% endhint %}

### Issuing cards

Go to **Cards** to issue virtual or physical Visa cards to employees. You can set per-card limits that override or reinforce the team policy.

### Connecting your accounting system

Payhawk supports direct integrations with:

| Platform | Sync type |
|----------|-----------|
| Xero | Real-time |
| QuickBooks Online | Real-time |
| NetSuite | Real-time |
| SAP Business One | Scheduled |
| Microsoft Business Central | Scheduled |
| Sage Intacct | Scheduled |

Go to **Settings → Integrations** to connect your accounting system and map Payhawk categories to your chart of accounts.
