# Opening New Payhawk Accounts

## Prerequisites

Before you start, have the following ready:

- Your company registration number and registered address
- A valid business bank account (for topping up your fund account)
- Details of the primary administrator (name, email, role)

{% hint style="info" %}
Payhawk supports companies registered in the EU, UK, and US. See [Payhawk in the US](payhawk-us.md) for US-specific requirements.
{% endhint %}

## Registration steps

{% stepper %}
{% step %}
## Start your application

Go to [payhawk.com](https://payhawk.com) and click **Get started**. Enter your company email and create a password.
{% endstep %}

{% step %}
## Verify your company

Enter your company registration number. Payhawk will pre-fill your registered details from the public business registry. Confirm or correct them.
{% endstep %}

{% step %}
## Complete KYB

Payhawk's onboarding team will request Know Your Business documents — typically a certificate of incorporation, proof of address, and ID for the beneficial owners. This typically takes 1–3 business days.
{% endstep %}

{% step %}
## Top up your fund account

Once approved, transfer funds to your Payhawk fund account via bank transfer. This balance is used to load employee cards.
{% endstep %}

{% step %}
## Invite your team

Go to **Settings → Users** and invite your employees, accountants, and other administrators.
{% endstep %}
{% endstepper %}

## What happens after registration

Once your account is active, your administrator can:

- Issue virtual and physical cards to employees
- Set spend policies and approval workflows
- Connect Payhawk to your accounting or ERP system

For next steps, see the [Administrator guide](../user-guides/administrators/README.md).
