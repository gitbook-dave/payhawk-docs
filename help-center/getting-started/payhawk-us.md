# Payhawk in the US

Payhawk is available for companies incorporated in the United States. This page covers what's different for US accounts compared to EU/UK.

## Supported features

| Feature | US availability |
|---------|----------------|
| USD corporate cards (Visa) | ✓ |
| USD fund account (ACH top-up) | ✓ |
| QuickBooks Online integration | ✓ |
| NetSuite integration | ✓ |
| Xero integration | Coming soon |
| Multi-currency cards | Coming soon |

## Card programme

US cards are issued under the Visa network. Physical cards are shipped to US addresses only. Virtual cards are available immediately upon approval.

{% hint style="warning" %}
US cards are denominated in USD only. International transactions are converted at the Visa exchange rate with a 1.5% foreign transaction fee.
{% endhint %}

## Tax handling

US accounts support:

- **Sales tax** tracking per expense (state + local rates)
- **W-9 / W-8** vendor collection for 1099 reporting
- **Mileage reimbursement** at the current IRS rate

## Supported states

Payhawk is currently available to companies registered in all 50 US states and the District of Columbia.

## Getting started

Follow the standard [Opening New Accounts](opening-accounts.md) flow. During KYB, you will be asked for your EIN (Employer Identification Number) instead of a EU company registration number.
